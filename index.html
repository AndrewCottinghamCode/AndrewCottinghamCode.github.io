Nayland

Beautiful suffolk living

Nayland is nestled along the banks of the picturesque River Stour, surrounded by lush green meadows and rolling hills. The village exudes a sense of timeless beauty and tranquility that will captivate your heart. One of the highlights of Nayland is its stunning architecture. The village is home to a collection of historic buildings, including the magnificent St. James' Church, which dates back to the 15th century. Its striking tower dominates the skyline, offering a glimpse into the village's rich past.
In Nayland, you'll also find a selection of charming shops, boutiques, and cozy tearooms. Treat yourself to some local crafts, indulge in delicious homemade treats, or simply enjoy a peaceful afternoon sipping tea while watching the world go by.
